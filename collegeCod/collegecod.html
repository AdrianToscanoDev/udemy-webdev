ABOUT US

College CoD is a collegiate competition born from talent and drive, built grassroots by students for students. Teams come from all over the US + Canada to compete in Call of Duty and revel in the experience and community. We provide an exemplary competitive experience for college students through our games, player engagement and pursuit of professional grade services and support.

In addition to our play, we focus on engaging and attractive content with innovative broadcasting tech, extensive website tools, and a team full of passion and talent. We bring an experience to both players and fans that rivals professional esports
